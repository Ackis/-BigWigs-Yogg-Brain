== What it does ==
Displays the health of Yogg-Saron's brain during Phase 2 for all raid members.

== Where to get it ==
*[http://www.wowace.com/projects/bigwigs-yogg-brain/files/ WoW Ace] - Often Beta quality
*[http://wow.curse.com/downloads/wow-addons/details/bigwigs-yogg-brain.aspx Curse ] - Most updated stable version

== How to get it to work ==
You need to enable it in the Big Wigs options.

=== Commands ===
There is no interface, it just works.
